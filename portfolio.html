NY Times Search

The New York Times Search searches the New York Times data base for articles based on a particular subject.

How to Search for Articles:
  1. Enter the subject that you want to use to conduct a search.
  2. Choose the number of articles that you would like the search to return. Minimum 1. Max 10
  3. The date is optional. Must be entered in the YYYYMMDD format.
  4. Hit enter to start search.

The results to the search will appear under Top Articles. The search results can be cleared by clicking the clear button.
